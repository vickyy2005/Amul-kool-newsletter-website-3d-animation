A Legacy of Purity

For decades, Amul has been synonymous with the purest milk in India. Our journey with Amul Kool started with a simple vision: to create a refreshing beverage that stays true to our roots while offering a modern twist.

The creation of our Rose flavor is an art. We carefully select the finest rose essences and blend them seamlessly with our signature creamy milk. The result is a drink that not only quenches your thirst but transports you to a serene, royal garden with every sip.
